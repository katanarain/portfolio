SEA TURTLE EGG INCUBATOR
[September 2022 - Present]

ABOUT DEVICE
A device meant to cool sea turtle nests until they reach a temperature range that will produce 100% male hatchlings to help rectify the sex ratio imbalance. The sex of a sea turtle is determined by the temperature of the sand in which the egg is incubated. Due to rising global temperatures, 99% of sea turtles born within the last 4 years have been female.

MY ROLE
Engineering consultant with our conservationist community partners. Ideated, modelled, built and tested the device with my engineering team. Ran temperature trials in-lab and in-field to verify thermal simulation data. Currently working on a DIY version of the device for developing countries to access the technology in a cost effective way.

SKILLS
Product Design | Thermal Analysis | Public Speaking

DIGITAL HOLOGRAPHIC MICROSCOPE
[May 2022 - September 2022]

ABOUT DEVICE
Worked on a device that will be deployed in the soil to constantly monitor the movement of bacteria, archaea, fungi and protozoa distribution. This way we can study their spatial patterns and interactions within the microbiome at a micrometer scale.

MY ROLE
